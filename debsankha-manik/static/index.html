I am Debsankha Manik, an undergraduate student majoring in physics at IISER Kolkata in West Bengal, India. Besides physics - my primary area of academic interest, I derive pleasure in learning about quite a few of other subjects including mathematics, programming, electronics, literature and history. I am a firm believer in free and open source software, especially GNU/Linux.

Various projects I have undertaken so far in the course of my undergraduate study are listed in the Projects page. In case of projects involving programming, the programs can also be found there. Some of the codes can also be found in my svn repository hosted at code.google.com.

Sometimes when my boredom with the everyday chores reaches a threshold level (or sometimes out of real necessity), I set out to do some arbitrary job (like creating this website) which do not always reach completion. Some of those informal 'projects', if you would call them, find their place of glory in the Junk page.

The Stat page displays some stats about either me or this website. The stats are generally updated by me using my IM client or a nautilus action, utilizing the 'Incoming XMPP Messages' service of appengine.

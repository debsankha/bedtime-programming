Who am I?

I am Debsankha Manik, an undergraduate student majoring in physics at IISER Kolkata in West Bengal, India. Besides physics - my primary area of academic interest, I like to dabble in quite a few other subjects including mathematics, programming, electronics, literature and history. I am a firm believer in free and open source software, especially GNU/Linux.

What's in this site?

Various projects I have undertaken since joining college are listed in the Projects page, along with the project reports and presentations I haven't yet lost. In case of simulation-oriented projects, links to the relevant programs are also there.

Sometimes when my boredom with the everyday chores reaches a threshold level, I set out to do some arbitrary tasks (like creating this website) , knowing full well that the outcome of those endeavours won't affect me in any possible way. More often than not those jobs involve programming. Codes born that way, along with the ones I wrote for various projects find their place of glory in the Junk page. Most of these codes are scattered across my github repositories, too.
